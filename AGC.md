# Apollo Guidance Computer (AGC)

Date: 2026-03-19

The Apollo Guidance Computer (AGC) was a digital computer for NASA's Apollo program. It featured a 2.048 MHz processor using RTL integrated‑circuit chips, 15‑bit words with parity, 2048 words of magnetic‑core RAM, and 36,864 words of core rope ROM. Power consumption ~55 W, weight ~70 lb (32 kg). Operated via the DSKY interface and programmed in AGC Assembly Language.
